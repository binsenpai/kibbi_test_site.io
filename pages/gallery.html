About floatr

floatr is a unique type of file hosting and URL shortening service, allowing you to pick from over 140+ domains and many are added frequently!

This way, you can upload your files and get a customizable domain, this allows you to:

> Pass messages and spread ideas in unique ways
> Use domains in humouristic ways for fun
> Make your links stand out for their originality uniqueness and possibly cleverness

...And if you don't care about the domains, at least you get convienent file hosting as all links are permanent and direct File uploading and URL shortening can be done though this website or ShareX Visit the home page to secure yourself an invite.
